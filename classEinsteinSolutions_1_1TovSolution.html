Detailed Description

TOV solver based on Lindblom's method.

Uses Lindblom's method of integrating the TOV equations from "Phase Transitions and the Mass-Radius Curves of Relativistic Stars" L. Lindblom, Phys.Rev. D58 (1998) 024008

Instead of integrating \(m(r)\) and \(p(r)\) ( \(r\)=radius, \(m\)=mass, \(p\)=pressure) Lindblom introduces the variables \(u\) and \(v\), with \(u=r^{2}\) and \(v=m/r\). The integration is then done with the log of the specific enthalpy ( \(\mathrm{log}(h)\)) as the independent variable.

Lindblom's paper simply labels the independent variable as \(h\). The \(h\) in Lindblom's paper is NOT the specific enthalpy. Rather, Lindblom's \(h\) is in fact \(\mathrm{log}(h)\).
